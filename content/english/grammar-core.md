## 動詞與時態
### 基本時態
- 現在簡單式
  - 常態／事實
  - S + V(s/es)
  - She drinks coffee every morning.
  - Water boils at 100°C.
- 現在進行式
  - 當下正在進行
  - S + am/is/are + V-ing
  - He is reading a book right now.
- 過去簡單式
  - 過去發生且結束
  - S + V-ed
  - They visited Paris last summer.
### 完成式
- 現在完成式
  - 過去延伸至現在
  - S + have/has + p.p.
  - 搭配 already / yet / since / for
  - I have lived here for ten years.
  - She has already finished the report.
- 過去完成式
  - 過去的過去
  - S + had + p.p.
  - 常見於 before / after / when
  - He had left before she arrived.
### 特殊動詞搭配
- 使役動詞
  - make / have + O + 原形
  - let + O + 原形
  - get + O + to-V
  - 被動：make + O + p.p.
  - My mom made me clean my room.
  - She had the mechanic fix her car.
  - He got his sister to help him.
- 感官動詞
  - see / hear / watch + O + 原形（完整動作）
  - see / hear / watch + O + V-ing（進行中）
  - I saw him cross the street.（看到全程）
  - I saw him crossing the street.（看到進行中）
- 連綴動詞
  - look / feel / seem / become + 形容詞
  - ⚠️ 不加副詞
  - The soup smells delicious.（不是 deliciously）
  - She looks tired today.
### 語態變化
- 主動語態
  - S + V + O
  - The chef cooked the meal.
- 被動語態
  - S + be + p.p. + (by + 施事者)
  - 時態由 be 動詞變化
  - The meal was cooked by the chef.
  - English is spoken worldwide.
  - 💡 強調受詞或施事者不重要時用被動

## 三大子句
### 名詞子句
- that 引導
  - 作主詞：That he lied is wrong.
  - 作受詞：I know that she left.
  - 作補語：The fact is that he failed.
  - ⚠️ 作主詞時 that 不可省略
- Wh- 疑問詞引導
  - 間接問句語序：wh- + S + V
  - what / where / when / why / how
  - whether / if（是否）
  - I don't know where she lives.
  - Please tell me what time it is.
  - 💡 間接問句不倒裝！
### 形容詞子句（關係子句）
- 限定用法
  - 無逗號，指定對象
  - The man who called is my uncle.
- 非限定用法
  - 有逗號，補充說明
  - My brother, who lives in Tokyo, is a chef.
  - ⚠️ 不可用 that
- 關係代名詞
  - who（人，主格）
  - whom（人，受格）
  - which（事物）
  - that（人或事物，限定用法）
  - whose（所有格）
- 關係副詞
  - where（地方）
  - when（時間）
  - why（原因）
  - This is the town where I grew up.
  - I remember the day when we first met.
  - 💡 關係副詞＝介系詞＋which
### 副詞子句
- 表時間
  - when（當…時）
  - while（當…同時）
  - after / before / until
  - When she arrived, the party had started.
  - While he was cooking, she set the table.
- 表原因
  - because（強因果）
  - since / as（已知原因）
  - He stayed home because he was sick.
  - Since you're here, let's begin.
- 表讓步
  - although / though / even though
  - ⚠️ 不可搭配 but 同用
  - Although it was raining, they went hiking.
- 表條件
  - if（若…）
  - unless（除非）
  - as long as（只要）
  - Unless you hurry, you'll miss the bus.
  - You can borrow it as long as you return it.

## 句型與修飾
### 分詞與分詞構句
- 現在分詞 V-ing
  - 主動或進行意義
  - 修飾名詞：the sleeping cat
- 過去分詞 p.p.
  - 被動或完成意義
  - 修飾名詞：a broken window
- 分詞構句簡化
  - 省略連接詞 + 相同主詞
  - 主動：V-ing...
  - 被動：p.p... / Being p.p....
  - 💡 主句與分詞構句主詞須一致，否則懸垂分詞
- with 附帶狀況
  - With + O + V-ing（主動）
  - With + O + p.p.（被動）
  - With his eyes closed, he listened.
### 假設語氣
- 與現在事實相反
  - If + S + 過去式, S + would/could + 原形
  - If I were you, I would study harder.
  - ⚠️ be 動詞一律用 were
- 與過去事實相反
  - If + S + had + p.p., S + would have + p.p.
  - If she had studied, she would have passed.
- 意志動詞省略 should
  - suggest / insist / demand + that + S + (should) + 原形
  - He insisted that she (should) leave.
  - 💡 原形動詞不隨主詞變化
### 倒裝句型
- 否定副詞放句首
  - Never / Seldom / Rarely + 助動詞 + S + V
  - Never have I seen such a sight.
- Only 放句首
  - Only then / Only when + 助動詞倒裝
  - Only when he arrived did we start.
- 💡 倒裝 = 疑問句語序（助動詞提前）

## 詞性與連接
### 動詞轉名詞
- 動名詞 V-ing
  - 習慣性、已發生的事
  - 搭配：enjoy / avoid / finish / mind + V-ing
  - 作主詞：Swimming is fun.
  - I enjoy reading novels before bed.
  - He avoided making eye contact.
  - She stopped talking.（停止說話）
- 不定詞 to-V
  - 目的、未發生的事
  - 搭配：want / plan / decide / hope + to-V
  - 作目的副詞：I study to pass.
  - She decided to move abroad.
  - He stopped to check his phone.（停下來看手機）
  - 💡 stop to V（停下來去做）vs. stop V-ing（停止做）
### 易混淆介系詞與連接詞
- Although vs. Despite
  - Although + 子句（S + V）
  - Despite / In spite of + 名詞／V-ing
  - Although he was tired, he kept working.
  - Despite the rain, they went out.
- Because vs. Because of
  - Because + 子句（S + V）
  - Because of + 名詞／V-ing
  - She cried because she was moved.
  - She cried because of the sad movie.
- ⚠️ 介系詞後不接完整子句
### 語意轉折詞
- 承接
  - Therefore / Thus / As a result（因此）
  - Consequently（結果）
- 轉折
  - However / Nevertheless（然而）
  - On the other hand（另一方面）
- 遞進
  - Moreover / Furthermore / In addition（此外）
  - Besides（而且）
- 💡 轉折詞後加逗號，連接詞後不加
